What tax year?

Any tax is based on your income for the tax year. A tax year runs from 6 April to the following 5 April. You can use this checker to work out your tax as far back as the tax year 2015/2016 (6 April 2015 to 5 April 2016).

Here is an example covering two tax years:

  • you start work on 2nd March 2016
  • you work 20 hours a week for 7 months (29 weeks)
  • you earn a total of £3,770 (£130 a week)
  • your job falls into 2 tax years: 4 weeks in 2015/2016 and 25 weeks in 2016/2017
  • your employer gave you a form P60 showing pay of £520 and tax of £104.00 for the tax year 2015/2016
  • when you left he gave you a form P45 showing pay of £3,250 and tax of £650 for the tax year 2016/2017

Assuming you had no other jobs between 6 April 2015 and 5 April 2017, you will need to use the tax checker twice, once for 2015/2016 and once for 2016/2017.
